About Linda

The baker behind The Whimsical Hobbyist.

Hi, I'm Linda - the baker behind The Whimsical Hobbyist.

I'm a firm believer that life is sweeter when it's filled with hobbies, homemade treats, and a little bit of whimsy. Whether I'm developing new vegan dessert recipes, getting lost in a good book, tending to my garden, or spending time with horses, I love finding joy in the simple things.

Here you'll find approachable, plant-based baking recipes made with everyday ingredients, plenty of chocolate, and a sprinkle of nostalgia. My goal is to help you create beautiful homemade bakes that are as fun to make as they are to share.

So grab your apron, preheat the oven, and let's make something delicious together. Welcome to my little corner of the internet - I'm so happy you're here.
